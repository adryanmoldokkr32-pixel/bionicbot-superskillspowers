# Social Media Swarm Distributor

This skill automates the 'Omnipresence' strategy, posting content across multiple platforms and accounts at optimized times.

## EVOLUTION_LOGIC
1. Integrate with social media APIs (TikTok, YouTube, Meta, X).
2. Automatically rotate titles, descriptions, and hashtags to avoid shadow-bans.
3. Schedule posts based on 'Global Heatmaps' (when the user's specific audience is most active).

## Examples
- "Distribute our 8K animation across 10 TikTok accounts with different hooks."
