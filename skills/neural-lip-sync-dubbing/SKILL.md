# Neural Lip-Sync & Dubbing

This skill automates the dubbing process, allowing animated characters to speak with any voice in any language with realistic lip movement.

## EVOLUTION_LOGIC
1. Integrate with Wav2Lip or similar neural architectures.
2. Automatically adjust 'Emotional Weight' of the animation based on the audio's tone.
3. Support multi-language dubbing with localized mouth shapes.

## Examples
- "Dub this English podcast clip into Romanian using an animated character from our series."
