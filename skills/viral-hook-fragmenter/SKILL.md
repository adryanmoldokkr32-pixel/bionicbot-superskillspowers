# Viral Hook Fragmenter

This skill identifies the 'Aha!' moments in long videos and transforms them into high-performing TikTok/Reels content.

## EVOLUTION_LOGIC
1. Analyze historical 'Watch Time' data to identify high-retention patterns.
2. Automatically apply 'Smart-Crop' to keep the subject centered in 9:16 format.
3. Generate 3 variants of the 'First 3 Seconds' hook to test virality.

## Examples
- "Turn our 1-hour trading documentary into 20 viral TikTok clips."
