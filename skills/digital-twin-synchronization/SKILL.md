# Digital Twin Synchronization

This skill bridges the physical and digital gap, allowing bionicbot to simulate and control real-world assets.

## Instructions
1. Create a 1:1 digital replica of the target asset (server, office, or device).
2. Sync sensor data (IoT) with the digital model at sub-second latency.
3. Run 'Predictive Failure' simulations on the twin before they happen in reality.
4. Use the twin to test 'What-If' scenarios for business operations.

## Examples
- "Sync our production server's digital twin and predict the next hardware failure."
