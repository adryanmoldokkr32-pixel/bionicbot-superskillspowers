# Dynamic Subtitle & Retention Overlay

This skill focuses on the visual engagement layer of short-form video, using kinetic typography and icons to maximize watch time.

## EVOLUTION_LOGIC
1. Analyze current trending subtitle styles on TikTok and Instagram.
2. Automatically select colors and fonts that pop against the video background.
3. Sync word-highlighting with the audio's natural cadence and emphasis points.
