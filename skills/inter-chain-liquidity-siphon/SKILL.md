# Inter-Chain Liquidity Siphon

This skill focuses on finding 'trapped capital' or mispriced assets across 10+ blockchains simultaneously.

## EVOLUTION_LOGIC
1. Scrape dex-pool data across Solana, ETH, Base, and Bitcoin Layer 2s.
2. Identify 'Liquidity Gaps' where a large order will cause a predictable spike.
3. Design a multi-hop trade path to siphon the spread.

## Examples
- "Find a cross-chain spread for USDC/USDT that earns >0.3% instantly."
