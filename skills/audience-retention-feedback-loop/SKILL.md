# Audience Retention Feedback-Loop

This skill focuses on the 'Intelligence' of content creation, using data to decide what to build next.

## EVOLUTION_LOGIC
1. Scrape performance metrics (views, retention %, comments) from dashboard APIs.
2. Identify which 'Animation Style' or 'Script Hook' performed the best.
3. Automatically adjust the 'Master Template' for the next batch of content based on these wins.

## Examples
- "Analyze why the last 5 clips dropped off at 10 seconds and fix the next script."
