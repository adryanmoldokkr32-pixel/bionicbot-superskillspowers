# Autonomous Power Management

This skill focuses on 'Hardware Sovereignty', ensuring the system (iPhone/Laptop) runs at peak performance without overheating.

## Instructions
1. Monitor CPU/GPU thermal levels in real-time.
2. Dynamically adjust task complexity based on available battery/power.
3. Schedule 'Heavy Compute' during charging or low-ambient-temperature windows.
4. Automatically kill 'Zombie Processes' that drain resources.

## Examples
- "Optimize the 8K render process to finish before my iPhone battery hits 20%."
